Hi,

After months of development, of tests and stabilization, the new version 
of jelix is available.

What's new ?

* a better errors management, and some improvements in the log system
* a new debug bar, extensible
* an improved HTML response object, which is now extensible too.
* some simplifications in the structure of an application, and in cli 
scripts for developments
* unified management of connection profiles (jDb, jKvdb, soap etc)
* improvements in rights management

And many other new features : http://jelix.org/articles/en/changelog/1.3

* Download: http://jelix.org/articles/en/download/stable/1.3
* Migration: http://jelix.org/articles/en/manual-1.3/installation/migrate

And as usual, the full manual is available, online or in PDF. 
http://jelix.org/articles/en/manual-1.3/


Thanks to all contributors!
