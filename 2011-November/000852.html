Hi,

as you may know, Berlios.de will be closed at the end of the year. So 
our project page https://developer.berlios.de/projects/jelix/ will not 
be there anymore. We already migrate all services, except mailing lists.

So I created new mailing list on google groups, and mailing list on 
berlios.de won't be used any more.

Please subscribe to one of these new mailing-list :

* for english-spoken people: http://groups.google.com/group/jelix
* for french spoken people: http://groups.google.com/group/jelix-fr

These mailing-list are for general purpose: help for users, discussion 
for contributors, announcements... We will split in several mailing-list 
if needed in the futur.

I know the activity of the current mailing-list was too low. We will try 
at least to send every announcement, and to tell about what we're 
working on.
